Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

Your Privacy is Protected: LitReview does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At LitReview, we believe your research activity is private. We have designed our application to respect your privacy completely. This privacy policy explains how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information: We don't collect your name, email, or any identifying information beyond what is required for payment processing
  • No search history: Your searches are not logged, tracked, or stored on our servers
  • No usage analytics: We don't track how you use the application
  • No tracking cookies: Our website and application do not use tracking cookies
  • No behavioral data: We don't monitor or collect information about your behavior

How the Application Works

LitReview is a desktop application that runs entirely on your local device. When you search for academic papers:

  • Searches are sent directly from your computer to Google Scholar
  • Results are processed locally on your device
  • All data, including search history and saved searches, is stored only on your computer
  • No information passes through our servers

Payment Processing

Payment for LitReview is processed securely through Stripe, a third-party payment processor. We do not store your payment information. Stripe may collect:

  • Payment card information
  • Billing address
  • Email address for receipt purposes

Stripe's use of your information is governed by their privacy policy, which can be found at stripe.com/privacy.

Third-Party Services

LitReview uses Google Scholar to retrieve search results. When you use the application:

  • Your searches are sent directly to Google Scholar from your device
  • Google may collect information according to their privacy policy
  • We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Search history
  • Saved searches and folders
  • Application preferences

You have complete control over this data and can delete it at any time by removing the application from your device.

Children's Privacy

LitReview does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The application is designed for academic research purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we don't collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for us to:

  • Provide to you upon request
  • Correct or update
  • Delete from our systems
  • Transfer to another service

Your data stays on your device, under your control.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
